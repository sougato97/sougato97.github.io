Research Interests

My interest is on SLAM systems. The OPEN VINS SLAM became a gateway for understanding these systems as this is the first paper I started reading and working on. The more I dived deep, I undertsood that SLAM systems are a universe of its own and can be implemented using different methods. These different methods results in different characterization.

The deep learning implementaion in SLAM systems is a bit new and has huge potential. To understand this implementation DROID SLAM turned into the gateway for me. While reading this paper I saw a strking fearure that this is far mmore robust than most of the systems but presently its lacking in real-time performance. I would like to dive deep and see how can we furthur improve these kind of implementations.

The field of image processing also fascinates me, for which I am engaged in the research of Dorsal Hand Vein as a biometrics. Here we have extracted the vein patterns and fed to different Neural Networks for training. These trained networks are used for authentication scenarios. We also are engaged in creating our own image-capturing setup so that we can have our own database and make a multi-modal biometrics authentication system.
